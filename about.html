The (AMSZ) is an affiliate of The Chamber of Mines of
Zimbabwe and was formed in 1985 as a Professional
Non-Profit Body that represents the interests of the
Mine Surveying Profession in Zimbabwe.It comprises
of members from Mine Surveying Departments
of various Mining Organizations, both locally and
abroad, as well as other affiliate members from diverse
technical fields related to Mine Surveying and the
Mining Industry in general.

Our Vision

To be a distinguished, world-class
professional organization that upholds
quality Mine Surveying to the Industry.

Our Mission

To represent, promote, enhance and
continuously improve the science and
practice of the Mine Surveying Profession
in order to maximize value for members,
the mining industry and all stakeholders.

Our Values

1. honesty
2. integrity
3. sustainability
4. innovation
5. transparency
6. accountability
7. mutual respect
